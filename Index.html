My Journey

Learning through redirection.

After completing my Class 10 and 12 education with a science background, I initially prepared for the NEET examination.

Despite dedicated preparation over two years, I was unable to clear the exam. That phase was challenging, but it gave me time to reflect seriously on my interests, strengths, and the kind of work I wanted to pursue long term.

Through that reflection, I realized my natural inclination was toward logical problem-solving, technology, and building systems. This clarity led me to make a conscious shift into the field of Information Technology by enrolling in a B.Sc. IT (Honours) program.

The transition was not easy. In my first year, coding concepts were unfamiliar and often difficult to grasp. Instead of giving up, I focused on strengthening fundamentals, practicing consistently, and learning through mistakes.

By my second year, concepts started to click. Programming, data handling, and analytical thinking became clearer, and I began enjoying the process of learning and problem-solving.

Today, I am a second-year student actively building skills in AI, Machine Learning, and data-driven development, focused on translating theory into real, deployable projects.
